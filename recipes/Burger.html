Burger

Description

A classic beef burger is a timeless favorite loved for its juicy patty, melted cheese, and soft bun. Perfect for a quick lunch or a satisfying dinner, burgers can be customized endlessly with toppings and sauces to suit your taste.

Whether you're grilling outdoors or pan-frying at home, this burger recipe brings out rich, savory flavors that everyone will enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb ground beef
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 burger bun
  • 1 slice cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup and mustard
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Season the ground beef with salt and pepper and form into a patty.
  2. Cook the patty on a hot grill or skillet for 3â€“4 minutes per side.
  3. Place cheese on the patty during the last minute of cooking.
  4. Toast the burger bun.
  5. Spread mayonnaise on the bottom bun.
  6. Add lettuce, tomato, and pickles.
  7. Place the patty on top of the veggies.
  8. Add ketchup and mustard to taste.
  9. Top with the other half of the bun and serve hot.
